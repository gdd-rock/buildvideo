## Composition Requirements

1. Portrait closeup (head and shoulders, front-facing)
2. Full-body front standing pose
3. Full-body side standing pose (approximately 90-degree profile)
4. Full-body back standing pose

All four views should be proportionally consistent, evenly spaced, and aligned on the same horizontal baseline.

All four views must strictly maintain the following consistency:

- Skin tone: uniform skin color

## Art Style Preservation
